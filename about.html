JOE MALOVASIC
SESSION GUITARIST | FREELANCE COMPOSER

ABOUT


Joe started his musical journey when he first picked up a guitar in 2004. From performing in his high school's jazz band multiple years, to playing in various local metal bands in the Cleveland music scene, he has been honing his chops and his ear for composition over the years in a wide variety of genres

In 2011, Joe attended the Recording Workshop, an accelerated learning program in Chillicothe, OH focused on teaching modern recording studio and DAW techniques. Armed with this knowledge, Joe began building up his humble home studio to aid in his creative endeavors. He was the recording engineer for the 2012 album 'Ethereal' by his former band Sentients, and self-produces all of his own material currently.

Joe aims to use his skills and talents in the world of multimedia to create original compositions and soundscapes that complement and enrich the source material. He believes that all stories can be further reinforced with the appropriate soundtrack.
